How it Works ?

  • Newton is built around three things: people, processes, and connectivity. We've been working tirelessly, researching, implementing and stabilizing the underlying technology which does all the awesome stuff.

  • Billions of transistors switching perfectly, those electrons exactly where they are needed, thousands of function calls executing the way they should. So many man-years of code and hardware design, gets worked up within an instant. Your house gets its own nervous system.

  • It starts by converting your raw physical environment into a digital ecosystem using sensors and actuators. Current sensors for appliances, notification sensors for doors and windows, IR blasters for TVs and air conditioners, environment sensors for motion, temperature, pressure and humidity; networked LED bulbs, video cameras and door locks.

  • The sensors are networked using IPv6 over 802.15.4 to a local gateway then and connected to the Internet via the WiFi router. This gives the advantage to operate in your personal area without Internet connectivity and remote access via the Internet.
